# Diagnostic equations

## Dimensional diagnostic equations

mass of capillary-trapped CO<sub>2</sub>

$$m_C=\int_{\Omega}\varphi\varrho s~\text{d}\Omega$$

mass of dissolved CO<sub>2</sub>

$$m_D=\int_{\Omega}\phi(\rho(c,\theta)-\rho(0,\theta))~\text{d}\Omega$$

solutal flux

$$F=\frac{1}{|\Gamma|}\int_\Gamma(\textbf{u}c-\nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}\cdot\nabla c))\cdot\textbf{n}~\text{d}\Gamma$$

thermal flux

$$Q=\frac{1}{|\Gamma|}\int_\Gamma(\textbf{u}\theta-\nabla\cdot(\mathsf{G}\cdot\nabla \theta))\cdot\textbf{n}~\text{d}\Gamma$$
